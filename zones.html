Single-Zone System

  • Controls one group of tyres with a shared pressure setting
  • Ideal for drive-axle-only applications
  • Suits rigid trucks, off-road vehicles, and tractors
  • A simple, reliable solution for consistent loads or terrain

Dual-Zone System

  • Independently manages two tyre groups at different pressures
  • Commonly used for steer & drive axles, or drive & trailer
  • Supports internal or external air delivery to steer axles or trailers
  • Added flexibility for changing weights or conditions

Tri-Zone System

  • Full independent control over three axle groups
  • Typically used for steer, drive, and trailer sets
  • Fine-tuned pressure across complex combinations
  • Built to handle varied loads and mixed terrain with ease
